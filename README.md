# Thymatic Health

Thymatic Health is the AI coach for everyday mental and physical wellbeing—helping with stress, overwhelm, and emotional reset while also checking posture and exercise form—so it can hear how you feel, watch how you move, and guide you in real time with truly personalized support.

What makes it special is how much it brings together in one smooth experience: live voice intelligence, safety-aware emotional insight, and movement analysis. **Speechmatics** makes the live session feel instant with realtime partial and final transcription over streaming audio, while **Thymia Sentinel** adds policy-based wellbeing and safety analysis with confidence, concern, and urgency signals during the conversation itself. You can shift from talking to the coach to uploading an exercise clip for posture and form scoring, and it still feels like one continuous, human-centered system.

It is not just tracking symptoms or counting reps. Thymatic Health is designed to feel present, responsive, and reassuring, like a coach that can hear how you are doing, see how you are moving, and guide you in the moment.

## Demo workflows

### Workflow 1: Live voice coaching

**Example moment:** A user opens the app and says, *"I have been feeling tense all day and I cannot seem to switch off."*

1. On the home screen, click **Start Voice Session**.
2. The live session opens, the mic activates, and the orb shifts from ready to listening.
3. As the user speaks, their words begin appearing in the transcript stream in real time.
4. **Speechmatics** streams realtime partial and final transcripts, which is why the words appear live instead of waiting for the user to finish speaking.
5. **Thymia Sentinel** analyses the session as it unfolds and returns structured policy results like confidence, concerns, and urgency, which is why the Sentinel badges can update during the conversation.
6. The coach replies with spoken audio and live captions, so the experience feels immediate and human during a demo.
7. If the conversation suggests the user needs a gentler tone or more supportive guidance, the session adapts in real time instead of staying generic.

**What the audience sees:** live transcription powered by Speechmatics, live wellbeing monitoring from Thymia Sentinel, visible safety badges, and a coach that answers back with both voice and captions.

### Workflow 2: Higher-urgency support with a calming visual

**Example moment:** A user says, *"I feel overwhelmed and my chest is tight. I do not know how to calm down."*

1. Start a voice session as normal.
2. Let the user describe how they feel in their own words.
3. As **Thymia Sentinel** detects stronger concern signals, the Sentinel area reflects that with more prominent event badges and clearer urgency cues.
4. The coach responds with a calmer, more grounding reply, for example guiding the user through breathing or a gentle reset exercise.
5. Thymatic Health can also surface a supportive visual during the session, making the response feel more soothing and easier to follow in a live demo.
6. If you want to show the visual feature manually, click **Generate exercise visual** after the coach has replied.

**What makes this feel unique:** the app is not only "chatting." Speechmatics keeps the interaction live, while Thymia Sentinel gives the product a real safety and wellbeing layer that can influence how the coach responds in the moment.

### Workflow 3: Movement analysis after the conversation

**Example moment:** After the voice session, the user says they want help checking their knee rehabilitation exercise.

1. Return to the home screen and click **Analyse Movement**.
2. Upload a short video clip of the exercise.
3. Choose the joint to inspect, such as **left knee**.
4. Set the target angle, such as **90°**.
5. Click **Analyse movement**.
6. Thymatic Health processes the video, detects the user’s pose frame by frame, and extracts the observed joint angle.
7. The app then scores that movement against the target and returns both a numerical score and plain-language feedback.

**What the audience sees:** this is not a separate tool bolted onto the side. The same product that can talk with empathy can also review form and give actionable movement guidance.

### Workflow 4: End-to-end story in one demo

**Example scenario:** A user says, *"I have been stressed, I slept badly, and my knee feels stiff after physio."*

1. Begin with **Start Voice Session** and let the user explain how they feel.
2. Show the transcript building live as they speak.
3. Highlight that **Speechmatics** is handling the live transcript while **Thymia Sentinel** is reading wellbeing and safety signals from the same session.
4. Let the coach answer with calm spoken guidance and captions.
5. Optionally generate a visual to reinforce the recommended exercise or calming technique.
6. Then switch to **Analyse Movement** and upload the user’s rehab clip.
7. Score the knee movement against a target angle and show the feedback card.

**Why this lands in a live demo:** it shows one continuous care experience, from emotional check-in to practical physical guidance, instead of splitting voice support and movement support into different products.
